Outline your IT experience (if any).   Information Technology has played a vital role in my life. As a 90’s kid, I grew up with the first affordable computers and video game consoles. The internet broke through and my interest in IT was sparked. At that time, I had my first experiences HTML & PHP. With the vast development of IT within the last 15 years (introduction of the iPhone, HighSpeed Internet, Cloud Computing, automatisation, IoT & MachineLearning) and inspiring people like Steve Jobs, my view on IT shifted from being a fun and entertaining technology to being an opportunity for many people and companies to make their lives and work easier. However, I wasn’t going to be part of the IT development and I wasn’t going to help anyone, I was a user. I used IT as a main source of information, as a music producer and DJ and I acquired basic knowledge in HTML & CSS and eventually started to program or design small business websites and converted training material in a web-format for an IT-Training company. Furthermore, I used IT in my trade as an electritian and finally to manage a farm. While I used a lot of IT and researched for various solutions in different areas of life, I started to realise that I want to be part of the development and that I want to provide those solutions which I could not find or only fragments which would require to use many different applications. Most recently, I have started to learn Python and I’ve been working on my first projects with the Raspberry Pi. This was the first step towards creating solutions for myself.
